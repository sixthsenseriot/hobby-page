Why I love drawing

Whenever I am not learning anything and everything about coding, I spend my time doing digital art! Drawing or just simply doodling has been a great way for me to improve my mental health. I generally use it as an outlet from all of the worries and stress I carry in working to get my foot in the door in the tech industry. It's something that I am extremely passionate about even if I am losing sleep over it!

I primarily enjoy drawing characters from a book series called The Locked Tomb by Tamsyn Muir. Above all else, I love sharing my art with others that share the same interest in the series as well as supporting the creative content of different people in the community!

- Khanh Nguyen

More about me

Hello! My name is Khanh and I am an aspiring front-end developer! First and foremost, I really hope you like my Hobby Landing Page! SheCodes has been a great place for me to learn and code all while challenging the skills that I've obtained.

For many years, art has been a very important hobby of mine so much so that I cannot imagine life without it! I've been meaning to integrate my hobby into coding for the longest time but never seemed to have the chance or motivation to until now. Nowadays, I'm not letting myself draw that much anymore because I am spending most of my time trying to secure a position in the tech industry. It's tough out here, but I am dedicated to succeed! ヽ(。＞＜)ノ

Anyway, I look forward seeing all of the cool projects everyone in the SheCodes community has to offer for this challenge! I am actively trying to grow my connections and network so please feel free to shoot me a request on LinkedIn!— It would mean a lot to me!

That seems to be all I have to say regarding myself, but to anyone reading this far: I hope you are doing well and on top of that I hope you are successful in your endeavors whatever they may be! (´,,•ω•,,) ♡
